SEAL.jl

SEAL.jl is a Julia package that wraps the Microsoft SEAL library. It exposes the homomorphic encryption capabilitites of SEAL in an intuitive and Julian way.

Authors

SEAL.jl was initiated by Michael Schlottke-Lakemper, who is also the principal developer of SEAL.jl.

License and contributing

SEAL.jl is licensed under the MIT license (see License). Since SEAL.jl is an open-source project, we are very happy to accept contributions from the community. Please refer to Contributing for more details.
